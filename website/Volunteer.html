Volunteering with the Oakland Municipal Band

During the concert season, we need help setting up the bandstand before the concerts, and putting the chairs, music stands and other equipment away after the concert. If you would like to help with the setup, please come to the bandstand at 10:00 AM on the day of the concert. If you can stay after the concert, please help putting things away. Many hands make light work, and our organization is good, so this goes quickly and easily if we have enough people.


The rest of the year, we need help with:

  1. •Publicity

  2. •Outreach

  3. •Fund-raising

  4. •The web.


All donors to Friends of the Oakland Municipal Band become members and are invited to participate in our meetings. Donors of $100 or more are voting members. Help keep the band playing! For more information, contact us.
